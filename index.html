Do you know a lot about animals?

Why are animals important? Animals play an essential role in maintaining a healthy environment. Among other ways, they help with pollination, pest control, and climate control.

Domestic cat

House cats are obligate carnivores, meaning they have to eat meat to stay healthy.

As mostly nocturnal animals, cats have excellent vision and hearing, with ears that can turn like satellite dishes.

Cats are masters at communicating with other cats and their human caregivers.

When cats rub their faces on the furniture, other cats, or a person, it may be a behavior called bunting.

Like humans, cats display a preference for a particular paw, with males more often favoring their left paw and females their right.
-Animal Behaviour
